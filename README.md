# NoterHaat

Welcome to NoterHaat, the premier marketplace for bank notes with unique serial numbers. Our platform is designed for collectors who are passionate about buying, selling, and trading rare and uniquely numbered bank notes.

## Features
- **Buy and Sell**: Easily list your collection or find unique bank notes to add to your collection.
- **Trade**: Connect with fellow collectors to trade bank notes with distinctive serial numbers.
- **Community**: Join a vibrant community of bank note enthusiasts and collectors.

At NoterHaat, we celebrate the art and significance of bank notes, offering a user-friendly environment to engage in the world of currency collection.
